Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

What are your delivery hours and areas?

We deliver from 8:00 AM to 9:30 PM daily. We cover all areas within Mumbai city limits with a minimum order of ₹200. Delivery charges start at ₹30 depending on distance. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 8:00 PM.

Do you offer catering services for events?

Yes! We offer custom catering packages for corporate events, weddings, parties, and other special occasions. Our minimum order is 50 servings, and we provide everything including dispensers, cups, and staff. Contact our catering team at partnerships@juicejunction.com for a custom quote.

Are your juices 100% natural and fresh?

Absolutely! All our juices are made from 100% fresh fruits and vegetables with no added sugar, preservatives, or artificial flavors. We prepare each order fresh and recommend consumption within 2 hours for optimal taste and nutrition.

What is your refund/cancellation policy?

You can cancel your order free of charge up to 30 minutes before the scheduled delivery time. For quality issues, we offer a full refund or replacement within 2 hours of delivery. Just contact our customer support at +91 93333 44557 with your order details.

Do you have loyalty programs or discounts?

Yes! Join our Juice Club for 10% off all orders, free delivery on orders above ₹500, and exclusive access to new flavors. Corporate clients can avail special bulk discounts. Follow us on Instagram for weekly promotions and seasonal offers.
